📚 LightCraft Documentation
🎨 Getting Started

Import Photos: Click "Import Photos" button or drag & drop images onto the canvas.

Supported Formats: JPEG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC (auto-converted), TIFF

Navigation: Use zoom controls (bottom) and navigator panel (left) for precise editing.

🎭 Advanced Masking System

Brush Mask: Paint adjustments on specific areas. Adjust size, feather, and opacity.

Radial Mask: Create circular/elliptical masks. Click and drag to define area.

Gradient Mask: Linear graduated filters. Perfect for skies and horizons.

Object Selection: AI-powered subject detection. Click to auto-select objects.

Tip: Combine multiple masks for complex adjustments.

💾 Export & Presets

Export Image: Click "Export" button to download edited photo as JPEG.

Export Preset: Click 💾 icon to save settings as Lightroom-compatible .xmp file.

Batch Export: Click 📦 icon to process all images with current settings.

Memory: Settings auto-save per image. Reload and your edits are preserved!

⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl+E - Export Image

Ctrl+S - Save Template

Ctrl+Z - Reset Adjustments

\ - Toggle Before/After

M - Toggle Masking Tools

📊 Technical Tools

Histogram: RGB, Luminance, and Channels modes for exposure analysis.

Waveform Scope: Professional brightness distribution monitor.

Vectorscope: Color distribution and hue analysis.

RGB Parade: Individual channel level monitoring.

Zone System: Ansel Adams exposure zone reference.

🤖 Smart Tools

Auto Enhance: AI-powered one-click enhancement based on image analysis.

Smart Randomizer: Creative random adjustments within intelligent ranges.

Templates: One-click professional presets (Portrait, Landscape, etc.).

Recommendations: Context-aware suggestions based on image characteristics.
